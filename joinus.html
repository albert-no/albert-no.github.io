Join the lab

Opportunities

We welcome motivated graduate students, postdocs, and interns who want to work on privacy, robustness, and generative AI.

Openings

We are looking for passionate and self-motivated undergraduate interns, graduate students, and postdocs to join our lab.

Key qualifications

  • Strong background in probability, statistics, and linear algebra.
  • Practical experience implementing and training deep learning models.
  • Strong communication skills and personal integrity.
  • Background in real analysis, advanced probability theory, or convex optimization is highly desirable.

Getting involved

When you write a contact email to Prof. Albert No, please include the following information:

  • Transcript
  • CV
  • Short justification of your qualifications

Regretfully, we may not be able to respond to most inquiries regarding graduate or postdoctoral openings.

Please send your materials to albertno@yonsei.ac.kr.
