Thesis

  • Rateless Lossy Compression via the Extremes (Advisor: prof. Tsachy Weissman)
    The aim of my thesis research is to understand successive refinement more thoroughly so that we can use its ideas to construct a practical lossy compressor with theoretically attractive properties. I proposed an iterative lossy compressor achieving the information theoretic optimum distortion-rate bound with low computational complexity, low storage requirements, and additional properties of both theoretical and practical merit.
